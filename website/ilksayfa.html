Personal School Overview

This project was initiated to help students follow the lessons more easily and make the communication between student and teacher easier and faster.

Announcments

When the teacher enters the system and makes a new announcement, the announcement will appear on the student's account.

Favorites

The student will be able to favorite and access the course in the program from the course list.

Notes
